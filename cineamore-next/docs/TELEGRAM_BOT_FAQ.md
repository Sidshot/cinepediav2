## ❓ FAQ

### Q: How do I download a movie?
1. Go to [cinepediav2.vercel.app](https://cinepediav2.vercel.app)
2. Search for your movie
3. Open the movie page
4. Scroll down to **Download Links**
5. Click your preferred quality (1080p/4K)

### Q: How do I stream a movie or series?
1. Open any movie/series page on CineAmore
2. Click the **Play** button on the poster
3. Choose a streaming server
4. Enjoy! 🎬

### Q: What content is available?
- **Movies**: 1000+ films from your personal library
- **Series**: Trending TV shows from TMDB
- **Anime**: Popular anime from Japan

### Q: Is it free?
Yes! 100% free. No ads, no subscriptions.

### Q: The bot isn't responding to my command
Make sure you're typing the command correctly:
- ✅ `/random` (correct)
- ❌ `random` (missing slash)

### Q: I found a bug or have a suggestion
Contact the admin directly!
